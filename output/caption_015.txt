Urdu hai jis ka naam hamin jaante hain 'Daagh'
Saare jahan mein dhoom hamari zabaan ki hai

— Daagh Dehlvi

Meaning: The language called Urdu — we know it well, Daagh. Our tongue is celebrated across the whole world.
