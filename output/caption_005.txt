Hui muddat ke 'Ghalib' mar gaya par yaad aata hai
Woh har ik baat pe kehna ke yoon hota to kya hota

— Mirza Ghalib

Meaning: It has been long since Ghalib died, yet one remembers his way of asking at every turn: what if it had been so?
