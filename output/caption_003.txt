Ishq par zor nahin hai ye woh aatish 'Ghalib'
Ke lagaye na lage aur bujhaye na bane

— Mirza Ghalib

Meaning: Love obeys no command, Ghalib — it is a fire that cannot be lit at will, nor extinguished by effort.
