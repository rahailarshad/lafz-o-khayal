Hazaaron khwahishein aisi ke har khwahish pe dam nikle
Bahut nikle mere armaan lekin phir bhi kam nikle

— Mirza Ghalib

Meaning: A thousand desires, each worth dying for. Many were fulfilled, yet they still feel too few.
