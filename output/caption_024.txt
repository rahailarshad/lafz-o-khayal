Ragon mein daudte phirne ke hum nahin qaail
Jab aankh hi se na tapka to phir lahu kya hai

— Mirza Ghalib

Meaning: Merely coursing through the veins does not impress me — if it never fell from the eye as tears, what kind of blood is it?
