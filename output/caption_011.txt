Khudi ko kar buland itna ke har taqdeer se pehle
Khuda bande se khud poochhe bata teri raza kya hai

— Allama Iqbal

Meaning: Elevate your selfhood so high that before writing every destiny, God Himself asks: tell me, what is your wish?
