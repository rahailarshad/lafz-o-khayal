Le saans bhi aahista ke naazuk hai bahut kaam
Aafaaq ki is kaargah-e-sheesha-gari ka

— Mir Taqi Mir

Meaning: Breathe softly, for delicate is the work of this glass-blower's workshop that is the universe.
