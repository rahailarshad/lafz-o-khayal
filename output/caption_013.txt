Nahin tera nasheman qasr-e-sultani ke gumbad par
Tu shaheen hai basera kar pahaadon ki chattanon mein

— Allama Iqbal

Meaning: Your nest does not belong on the dome of royal palaces. You are a falcon — make your home on mountain cliffs.
