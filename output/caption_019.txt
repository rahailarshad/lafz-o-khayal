Woh jo hum mein tum mein qaraar tha tumhein yaad ho ke na yaad ho
Wahi yaani waada nibaah ka tumhein yaad ho ke na yaad ho

— Momin Khan Momin

Meaning: That understanding between you and me — you may remember it or not. That very promise of faithfulness — you may remember it or not.
